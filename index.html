More dessert than drink, an affogato is sometimes served with a shot of liqueur.

An Americano is one of the few expresso-based drinks that doesn't contain milk.

A breve, also known as the 'American latte', contains a mixture of expresso, milk and cream.

Known for its frothy milk foam top, a cappuccino is not exceedingly strong.

Translated to 'cut' in Spanish, a cortado is served in a small glass, cut with very little, if any, froth.
